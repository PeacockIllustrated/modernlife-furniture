# CONTENT.md — Modern Life Furniture

## Voice
Gallery register with a restorer's warmth. Confident, quiet, concrete. Short declaratives with the occasional long, comma-joined sentence for rhythm. The brand does not sell; it curates and explains. Humour is dry and rare ("a Copenhagen apartment, a Northumberland farmhouse, and a very patient dog").

Hard rules: British spelling. No em-dashes (commas, semicolons, full stops). Sentence case headings. No exclamation marks. No emoji. Story paragraphs max ~36ch measure on desktop cards.

## Attribution rules (legal and ethical)
Never state a real designer or maker as fact for a piece without owner confirmation. Approved hedges: "attributed to", "school of", "in the manner of", "maker unconfirmed". Never write "after [designer]" marketing copy that implies authentication. Era and style descriptors (Bauhaus school, Danish modern, space age) are fine as categories.
